ELDRITCH EXPANSION ICONS

Place the following PNG icon files in this folder:

Required Files:
- icon_exp_fl.png  (Forsaken Lore)
- icon_exp_mom.png (Mountains of Madness)
- icon_exp_sr.png  (Strange Remnants)
- icon_exp_utp.png (Under the Pyramids)
- icon_exp_soc.png (Signs of Carcosa)
- icon_exp_td.png  (The Dreamlands)
- icon_exp_cir.png (Cities in Ruin)
- icon_exp_mon.png (Masks of Nyarlathotep)

Icon Size: 24x24 to 48x48 pixels recommended
Format: PNG with transparent background

Once you add these files, rebuild the app and the icons will appear
next to each expansion checkbox on the setup screen.
